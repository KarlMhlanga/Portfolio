Who are we?

Vast Exposure is a multidisciplinary design agency who specializes in web design, branding, graphic design and wedding stationery. Our innovative team of creatives are extremely passionate and thrive off transforming an idea into something striking, something beautiful.

We work closely with our clients to ensure all requirements are fully understood before an idea is created across all mediums. Vast Exposure provides the highest level of service and ongoing support even after the completion of a project.

We love what we do and are always up for a new challenge. We work globally but Pretoria, South Africa is our home.
