Wedding Stationery.

Why choose Us.

Vast Exposure's wedding stationery department is situated in the heart of Pretoria, Gauteng. We thrive on creating custom wedding stationery for each couple, where the vision of your special day is captured in design and reflects you as a couple perfectly.

Our company is proud to be able to offer world class wedding websites where you get to show off those stunning couple photos along with your story, a countdown to your big day, bridal party information, RSVP you name it! We are also able to register your domain name and host your website for you.

Send through some ideas you have in mind and we will translate your vision into something elegant-pretty, rustic-vintage, modern-playful, or any other style you'd prefer.
